Jasper Timeline

A Masterclass in What Happens When Your Product Is the Build

The Rise (2021–2022)

  • Founded January 2021
  • Built a UX layer on top of OpenAI's API for marketing copywriting
  • Reached $42.5M ARR in the first 12 months — 30% month-over-month
  • October 2022: raised a $125M Series A at a $1.5B valuation
  • Projected $140M ARR by end of 2022 and $250M by end of 2024

The Inflection (November 2022)

  • Everything Jasper did, ChatGPT could do for $20/month or free
  • Then Google Docs added AI writing. Notion added AI.
  • Every platform bundled the capability that was Jasper's entire product

Jasper Timeline

A Masterclass in What Happens When Your Product Is the Build

The Decline (2023–2024)

  • Jasper revised its ARR forecast down by at least 30%
  • The company conducted layoffs in July
  • Both co-founders stepped down
  • Internal valuation cut 20%
  • Revenue dropped from $120M to roughly $35–55M

The Attempted Pivot (2024–2025)

  • Revenue recovered to $88M — well below the $250M projected for 2024
  • Now positions itself as a "multi-agent marketing platform"
